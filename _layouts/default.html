How It Works

  1. Guest requests WiFi.
  2. Staff issues Square receipt, or guest enters purchase code into Captivi portal.
  3. Captivi validates purchase in real time, and grants secure WiFi access for a set duration.
  4. Guests browse, shop, or enjoy—worry free.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Does Captivi work with any WiFi system?
    Captivi is built specifically for Ubiquiti UniFi networks. We may expand to other platforms in the future—contact us if you have questions!
  • How does Square integration work?
    Captivi verifies guest WiFi access by checking for a valid Square receipt or code. You control the rules—such as how long access is granted, and whether refunds revoke access.
  • Is guest data safe and private?
    Yes! Captivi only verifies purchase and device information. We don’t store or sell guest browsing data, and you’re always in control.
  • Can I customize the captive portal?
    Absolutely. Add your logo, colors, and messaging for a seamless guest experience.
  • What support is included?
    All maintenance plans include remote updates, monitoring (on Premium), and email support. Phone or onsite support is available by request.
  • What if I need a network upgrade?
    Our team can help design, upgrade, or troubleshoot your UniFi environment—see our Network Design Services above.
